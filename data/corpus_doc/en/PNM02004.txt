kikunojo segawa
kikunojo segawa is a myoseki(professional name)of kabuki actor.
it was a great myoseki representing onnagata(female-role actor)of edo kabuki.
at present,the actor of zenshinza is calling himself the seventh kikunojo segawa.
its yago(stage family name)is hamamuraya.
its jomon(family crrst)is maru ni yuimon.
