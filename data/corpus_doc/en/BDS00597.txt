sanbujo
sanbujo is a gemon taken from hojisan(hymns of the pure land ritual).
gemon
奉請弥陀如来(bujomidanyorai)入道場(nyudojo)散華楽(sangeraku)
奉請釈迦如来(bujoshakanyorai)入道場(nyudojo)散華楽(sangeraku)
奉請十方如来(bujojipponyorai)入道場(nyudojo)散華楽(sangeraku)
translation
adored amida nyorai,please enter the dojo,where i will scatter flowers to receive you.
adored shaka nyorai(shakyamuni),please enter the dojo,where i will scatter flowers to receive you.
adored saints from everywhere,please enter the dojo,where i will scatter flowers to receive you.
