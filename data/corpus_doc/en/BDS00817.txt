enso
enso is a type of calligraphic technique in zen calligraphy,which is a circle painted with a single stroke.
it is also called as 'ichienso' or 'ensozu.'
it is thought to be a symbol of enlightenment,truth,bussho(the buddhist nature)or the whole universe,but the interpretation is left to each person.
also,sometimes the chinese characters円窓is used for enso,which means 'a window that shows your heart.'
