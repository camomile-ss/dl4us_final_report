mino no obayashi
mino no obayashi was a person of the nara period.
he lived in echizen province.
summary
he was the head of a family in usuna village,asuwa county,echizen province.
his kubunden(the farm land given to each farmer in the ritsuryo system)of 997.20 square meters in chimori village was transferred to the name of todai-ji temple in 766.
