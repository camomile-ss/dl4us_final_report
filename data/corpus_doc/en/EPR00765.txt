kibitsuhime okimi
kibitsuhime okimi(year of birth unknown-october 31,643)was in the imperial(royal)family in japan.
she was granted the honorific title "kibi no shima sumeramioya no mikoto".
her father was prince sakurai(son of emperor kinmei).
she married chinu no okimi(son of oshisaka no hikohito no oenomiko)and had takara no himemiko(empress kogyoku)and prince karu(emperor kotoku).
