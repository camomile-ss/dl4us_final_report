imperial prince fushimi-no-miya sadamochi
imperial prince fushimi-no-miya sadamochi(april 9,1760-july 20,1772)was a member of the imperial family who lived in the edo period.
he was the 17th head of the fushimi-no-miya family.
he was the second son of emperor momozono.
his mother was tomiko ichijo,a daughter of kaneka ichijo.
his childhood name was ninomiya.
in july 1760,he inherited the fushimi-no-miya family after the death of imperial prince fushimi-no-miya kunitada.
in 1763,he received the title of imperial prince.
he was named sadamochi.
on july 17,1772 he received the title of nihon(the second court rank for imperial princes);however,he passed away on 20 of the same month.
he was 13 years old.
his homyo(a posthumous buddhist name)was shinjomyoin.
