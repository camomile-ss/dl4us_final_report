miyabi
miyabi or masa(in case of name)means sophisticated and graceful.
courtly and urban.
and fine appearance.
personal and other names
雅is used for personal names relatively often and read 'miyabi' for female names in japanese reading and 'masa' for male names in phonetic reading.
trade names
雅(cigarette)(miyabi)
a brand name of cigarettes once sold.
miyabi(rail car)
a nickname of japanese style passenger car that was once used by japanese national railways.
