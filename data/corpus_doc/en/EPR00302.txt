sonkan
sonkan(1349-november 19,1400)was a priest of the ji sect during the period of the northern and southern courts(japan).
his father was emperor kameyama's prince,imperial prince tokiwainomiya tsuneaki.
career
1360:he entered into priesthood and lived in the kamakura shojoko-ji temple.
1369:he became chief priest of hyogo shinko-ji temple in settsu province.
1377:he became chief priest of yamagata komyo-ji temple in dewa province.
1385:he became chief priest of fuchu ichiren-ji temple in kai province.
1387:he succeeded to become the 12th chief priest of yugyo-ji temple in sagami province.
grave
sennen-ji temple in shimonoseki city,yamaguchi prefecture
