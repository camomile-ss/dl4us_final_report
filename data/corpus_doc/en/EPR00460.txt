ahiratsuhime
ahiratsuhime(吾平津姫)was the wife of emperor jinmu.
in "kojiki" (the records of ancient matters),she was mentioned as ahirahime(阿比良比売).
she was a younger sister of atanoobashi.
while emperor jinmu was living in hyuga province,she became the wife of emperor jinmu and had two sons,tagishimimi no mikoto and kisumimi no mikoto.
