hochi
hochi(years of birth and death unknown)was a priest in the nara period.
he was a disciple of ganjin(jianzhen).
soshichishu(seven monks)
career
it is said that he witnessed the construction of toshodai-ji temple by ganjin(jianzhen)at the old site of imperial prince niitabe in 759.
in december of 763,he returned the "shikan" (cessation and observation)sutra from todai-ji temple to toin(tang-in temple).
