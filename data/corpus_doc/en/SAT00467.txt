ryumyo-ji temple
ryumyo-ji temple is a temple of the nichiren-shoshu sect located in yamato-takada city,nara prefecture.
its sango(literally, "mountain name"),which is the title prefixed to the name of a buddhist temple,is hojusan.
origin and history
june 23,1988-founded
the patron of the temple in its founding was nikken shonin,the 67th head priest of nichiren-shoshu taiseki-ji temple.
address
18 asahi-kita machi,yamato-takada city,nara prefecture
surrounding area
asahi-kita post office
joko-ji temple pond
access
10 minute walk from takada station(nara prefecture)on sakurai line and wakayama line
