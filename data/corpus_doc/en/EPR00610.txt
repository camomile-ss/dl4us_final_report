imperial prince arisugawa-no-miya orihito
imperial prince arisugawa-no-miya orihito(august 19,1754-april 2,1820)was a member of the imperial family who lived in the edo period.
he was the seventh son of imperial prince arisugawa-no-miya yorihito.
he was the sixth family head of the arisugawa-no-miya family.
his children include imperial prince arisugawa-no-miya tsunahito,imperial prince soncho nyudo,princess takako and princess yoshiko.
princess takako was the lawful wife of the 12th tokugawa shogun,ieyoshi tokugawa.
princess yoshiko was the lawful wife of nariaki tokugawa,the lord of the mito domain,and the real mother of yoshinobu tokugawa.
