michinokuchinokihe no kunimiyatsuko
michinokuchinokihe no kunimiyatsuko道口岐閉国造(also known as michinokuchinokihekokkuzo)was kuninomiyatsuko(local ruling families in ancient japan)ruled the northern edge of hitachi province.
it is also written as道尻岐閉国造or岐閇国造.
summary
ancestor
takekoro no mikoto.
his son usahinone was appointed michinokuchinokuchinokihe no kunimiyatsuko during the reign of emperor ojin.
base
michinokuchi go,taka gun,hitachi province.
