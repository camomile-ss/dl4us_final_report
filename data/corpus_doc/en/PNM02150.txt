arata hosho
arata/shin hosho(november 16,1870-june 10,1944)was a noh actor of the hosho school of waki-kata(supporting actors).
the 10th head of the hosho school of waki-kata.
his real name was asataro hosho.
he was born in tokyo as a son of shinsaku hosho,the eighth head of the school.
kingoro eishu hosho,the ninth head of the school,was his uncle.
he distinguished himself through the meiji,taisho,and showa periods,and was celebrated as a master of noh.
he was known for teaching a noh chant to soseki natsume.
akihiko hosho is his son.
shigeyoshi mori is his son-in-law;tsuneyoshi mori is his grandson.
yaichi hosho is his second daughter's husband;kan hosho is his grandson.
