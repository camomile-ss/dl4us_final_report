nakachojamachi-dori street
nakachojamachi-dori street is one of the streets running from east to west in kamigyo ward,kyoto city.
this street is located between kamichojamachi-dori street and shimochojamachi-dori street,and extends approx.400m.
the area along nakachojamachi-dori street which is located off the arterial highway and slightly in the west of the kyoto gyoen national garden is a central part of an urban area where stores,offices and housings coexist.
