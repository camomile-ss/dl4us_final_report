cloistered imperial prince shoe
cloistered imperial prince shoe(1094-march 4,1137)was a shingon sect priest in the late heian period.
he was the fifth prince of emperor shirakawa.
he became a priest at ninna-ji temple and learned esoteric buddhism under joju-in kanjo.
he lived in kezo-in temple.
in 1127 sanbon was bestowed on him and in 1132 he prayed for the cure of emperor toba's disease.
his disciples included kangyo.
