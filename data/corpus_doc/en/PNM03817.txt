chohan kumasaka
chohan kumasaka was a legendary thief who was said to have lived during the heian period.
he was said to have been from kumasaka,echigo province.
it was said that he attacked ushiwakamaru at akasaka-jyuku station,mino province while ushiwakamaru was heading towards oshu(mutsu province)accompanied by kichiji kaneuri,but chohan was eventually defeated by ushiwakamaru.
people have adopted his story for both noh and kabuki performances.
