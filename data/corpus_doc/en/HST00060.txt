omiya
omiya is a venerable soy sauce store in kyoto.
the store was managed by shinsuke iguchi of omiya from iguchi family,a reputable family that owned lands in the area of present shijo kawaramachi.
it is also known as a place where ryoma sakamoto and shintaro nakaoka were assassinated(the omiya incident).
now a travel agency is located at the site of omiya,and only a stone monument remains in the place where sakamoto and nakaoka met a disaster.
a grave of the iguchi family,the master of omiya,is located in konkai komyo-ji temple.
