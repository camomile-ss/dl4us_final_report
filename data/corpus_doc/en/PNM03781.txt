fujiwara no masako
fujiwara no masako(dates of birth and death unknown)was a court lady in the kamakura period.
her father was masahira in hossho-ji temple.
her mother was a daughter of mitsuuji fujiwara.
masako served emperor kameyama and became naishi no suke(a court lady of the first rank)and was called chunagon naishi no suke(secretary of middle councilor).
she gave birth to imperial princess kishi(later shokeimon-in)and was appointed to jusanmi(junior third rank).
