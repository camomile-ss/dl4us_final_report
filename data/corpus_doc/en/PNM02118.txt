rokuro umewaka(the 54th)
rokuro umewaka,the 54th(april 28,1878-august 16,1959)was a nohgakushi(noh actor)playing shite(principal roles)of kanze-ryu school.
minoru umewaka,the second.
the second son of minoru umewaka,the first.
his older brother was manzaburo umewaka(the first).
he was adopted by his brother-in-law rokuro umewaka,the 53rd and succeeded to the rokuro umewaka family.
1948:retired and succeeded to minoru umewaka,the second.
1955:became a member of the japan art academy.
his first son was rokuro umewaka(the 55th).
