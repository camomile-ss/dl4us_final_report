kokuho chaseki san-meiseki(three national treasure teahouses)
three national treasure teahouses
tai-an teahouse at the myoki-an of daitoku-ji temple in yamazaki,kyoto
mitsu-an teahouse at the ryoko-in of daitoku-ji temple(kyoto city)
jo-an teahouse that was formerly located at kyoto's shoden-in of kennin-ji temple,but is now situated in the uraku-en garden of inuyama-jo castle
these are the three teahouses.
these are all considered to be the most valuable surviving teahouses in the history of japan's tea ceremony culture.
