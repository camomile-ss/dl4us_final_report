gato kataoka
gato kataoka is a kabuki(traditional drama performed by male actors)actor.
the stage family name is matsushimaya.
current gato kataoka is the fifth.
the jomon(family crest)is nanatsu-wari maru ni ni-hiki(two horizontal lines inside a circle that each has a width equal to one-seventh the diameter of the circle).
gato kataoka(片岡我當)is written as片岡我当in new letter shape.
