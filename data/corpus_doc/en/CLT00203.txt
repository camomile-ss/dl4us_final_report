the three largest festivals in kyoto
the three largest festivals in kyoto refer to the three festivals held in kyoto city,kyoto prefecture.
aoi matsuri festival(may)
a festival held in kamomiya-jinja shrine(shimogamo-jinja shrine)and kamowakeikazuchi-jinja shrine(kamigamo-jinja shrine).
gion matsuri festival(july)
a festival held at yasaka-jinja shrine.
it features yama-hoko junko,(float procession)and yoiyama(the eve of the main festival).
festival of the ages(october)
a festival held at heian-jingu shrine.
