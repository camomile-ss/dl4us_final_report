kan hosho
kan hosho(may 15,1934-)is a nohgakushi(noh actor)playing waki(supporting role)of the shimogakari-hosho-ryu school.
the present 12th head of the family.
his name kan hosho(宝生閑)is also written as寳生閑in chinese characters.
1934:born in tokyo as the oldest son of yaichi hosho and brought up in the house of his grandfather,arata hosho.
studied under his grandfather,his father and kenzo matsumoto.
1941:debuted in 'aoi no ue' (lady aoi).
1943:performed waki in 'iwafune' (a boat-like rock)for the first time.
1994:designated as individual holder of important intangible cultural property(living national treasure).
1996:received the medal with purple ribbon.
2002:assigned as a member of the japan art academy.
his oldest son is kinya hosho,a nohgakushi.
