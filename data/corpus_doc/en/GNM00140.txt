iwataki hot spring
iwataki hot spring is located in yosano-cho(former iwataki-cho)(tango province(old province)),yosa-gun,kyoto prefecture.
access
the nearest station is iwatakiguchi station on the miyazu line of kitakinki tango railway.
spring quality
earthy chloride,hydrogen sulfide springs
hot spring resort
the area has a hot-spring hotel,and a municipal hot-spring facility without accommodations, "qua house iwataki."
