genho
genho was a buddhist priest in the nara period.
he was a priest who studied in the tang dynasty.
summary
in 742,he went to yangzhou with yoei and fusho in order to return to their country.
they tried to bring jianzhen to the country,but got arrested with genro and others at kaiyuansi temple.
he iss said to have been released soon and returned to secular life.
