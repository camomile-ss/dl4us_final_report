hodo-ji temple(ikoma city)
hodo-ji temple is a yuzu nenbustu sect temple located in ikoma city,nara prefecture.
the sango(literally, "mountain name"),which is the title prefixed to the name of a buddhist temple,is ryugozan.
the principal image of buddha is jizo bosatsu(jizo bodhisattva).
history
although the year and details of its construction are unknown,it is said to have been founded by a monk called gyoki in the nara period.
at first,it was a shingon sect temple,however,it was later changed to a yuzu nenbustu sect temple during the bunsei era(1818-1830)in the late edo period.
