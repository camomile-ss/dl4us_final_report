renjo-ji temple(yasu city)
renjo-ji temple is a temple of the tendai shinseishu sect located in yasu city,shiga prefecture(former chozu-cho,yasu county).
sango(the temple's title)is seiryu-san.
the principal image is amida nyorai.
history
the temple is said to have been founded by zengen in 824 and restored during 1501-1504.
cultural property
important cultural property
wooden bishamon-ten ryuzo(wooden standing statue of the god of treasure,war)
address
1191,hie,yasu city,shiga prefecture
