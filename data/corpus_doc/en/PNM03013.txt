renzaburo suzuki
renzaburo suzuki(1848-october 6,1868)was a member of shinsengumi(a group who guarded kyoto during the end of tokugawa shogunate)from himeji,harima province.
lower commander of infantry.
he joined shinsengumi in 1867.
he served as a close retainer of isami konod,head of shinsengumi.
in the boshin war,he fought in the aizu war after fighting in the battle of toba-fushimi
he was killed on the field of the battle of bonari-toge.
died at 21.
