rakushisha
rakushisha(literally,a hut where persimmons fell)is a tea hut belonging to kyorai mukai,a follower of basho matsuo,located in sagano(ukyo-ku ward,kyoto city,kyoto prefecture).
the name of rakushisha comes from an incident when all of the persimmons,contracted to sell,fell due to a typhoon.
it is said that in 1691,basho matsuo stayed here and wrote the saga nikki(saga diary).
the present hut was the one reconstructed by juko inoue,a haiku poet.
location
2 saga ogurayama hinomyojin-cho,ukyo-ku ward,kyoto city,kyoto prefecture
access
20 minutes on foot from saga-arashiyama station on the jr sanin main line
