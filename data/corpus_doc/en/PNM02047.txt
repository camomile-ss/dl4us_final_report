takaie nigita
takaie nigita was a busho(japanese military commander),who lived during the period of the northern and southern courts.
he was the third son of masasue kusunoki and the nephew of masashige kusunoki.
in 1334,he constructed a castle at a place called 'kishi' (岸)by his uncle masanari's order,who was the head of the kusunoki clan.
it is said that this castle became to be called kishiwada(岸和田)-jo castle after the site's name '岸' composed with takaie's surname written as '和田.'
