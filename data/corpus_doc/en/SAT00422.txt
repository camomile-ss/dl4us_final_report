wakakusagaran
wakakusagaran are the remains of a buddhist temple discovered in the grounds of the south east of sai-in of horyu-ji temple in ikaruga-cho,ikoma county,nara prefecture.
summary
the tower and the kon-do hall are aligned on a same line which is similar to shitennou style garan alignment(the auditorium,corridors are not confirmed).
because of this,it is speculation that they are the remains of an ancient structure of horyu-ji temple,according to the article of the horyu-ji temple's fire destruction in 670 of the japanese chronicle of japan or destruction by fire by the attack of soga no iruka in 643.
