uchizane ichijo
uchizane ichijo(1276-february 15,1305)was kugyo(a court noble)in the kamakura period.
his father was ietsune ichijo.
his mother was the daughter of yoshitsugu matsudono.
his younger brother was nakasane ichijo and his child was uchitsune ichijo.
his go(pen name)was seishinin.
he was shonii(senior second rank).
brief personal history
he was born in 1276 as a son of ietsune ichijo.
he became jusanmi(junior third rank)in december 9,1288 and served as naidaijin(minister)(from 1302 to 1304.)
he died in 1304.
