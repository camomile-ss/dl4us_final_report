katsuhisa takayasu
katsuhisa takayasu(1948-)is a noh actor of the takayasu school of waki-kata(supporting actors).
the 14th head of the takayasu school.
the present head of the school.
he is a son of shigero nishimura,the 12th head of the school.
kinya nishimura,the 13th head of the school,is his uncle.
he is doing his activities mainly in nagoya.
