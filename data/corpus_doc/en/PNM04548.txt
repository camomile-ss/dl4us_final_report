mino no umakai
mino no umakai(馬甘)was a person of the nara period.
he has another given name,馬養.
his kabane(hereditary title)was mahito.
summary
he was actively involved in fujiwara no nakamaro's war and he was promoted from shorokuinojo(senior sixth rank,upper grade)to jugoinoge(junior fifth rank,lower grade).
although it is said that he had a son named mino no ishimori,he is considered to be a different person from the follower of otomo no tabito.
