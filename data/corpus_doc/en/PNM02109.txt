gasetsu kanze
gasetsu kanze(1898-december 7,1988)was a japanese nohgakushi(noh actor).
tetsunojo kanze,the seventh.
born in asakusa torigoe-cho as the fourth son of kosetsu kanze and a younger brother of kasetsu kanze.
in 1918,he took a name of orio.
in 1924,he married toshiya,kasetsu's adopted daughter.
in 1947,he succeeded to tetsunojo kanze,the seventh,and,building up a golden age of tessenkai(a noh group centering on the tetsunojo kanze family),in 1972 received the minister of education award for fine arts.
in 1979,he called himself gasetsu.
