princess yoshiko
princess yoshiko(years of birth and death unknown),was a member of the imperial family who lived in the early heian period.
she was an ise saigu(a vestal virgin princess serving at ise-jingu shrine).
she was a descendant of emperor kanmu,and her father was shikibukyo no miya(the highness of ceremonial),imperial prince nakano.
her mother was from the sugano clan.
in february 828,she was selected by her uncle,emperor junna,through divination to be a saigu,and traveled to ise after in september of 830.
in february 833,she resigned because of emperor junna's abdication of the throne.
