okazaki-michi station
okazaki-michi station was a railway station on the keihan keishin line of the keihan railway company.
it was located in higashiyama ward,kyoto city.
when the keishin line tracks were moved to sanjo-dori street,this station was closed.
history
august 15,1912:hiromichi station was opened when the keishin electric tramway began running.
july 1,1928:its name was changed to okazaki-michi station.
1931:the station was closed.
