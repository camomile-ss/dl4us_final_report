moritsuna tada
moritsuna tada(year of birth unknown-1156)was a japanese military commander of the late heian period.
he was the son of minamoto no yorinori(master of chamberlain),who was a courtier without post and belonged to tada-genji(minamoto clan).
he was also referred to as minamoto no moritsuna.
he accompanied his father yorinori,who was the servant of fujiwara no yorinaga,on the side of retired emperor sutoku during the hogen disturbance in 1156,but was captured and beheaded after losing the battle.
