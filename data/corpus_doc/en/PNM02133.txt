kyusen sakurama
kyusen sakurama(may 18,1889-march 1,1957)was a noh actor of the konparu school of shite-kata(lead actors).
his real name was kintaro sakurama.
he was the second son of banma sakurama.
michio sakurama was his cousin.
he was a leading noh actor of the konparu school in the taisho and showa periods.
kinataro sakurama was his eldest son.
