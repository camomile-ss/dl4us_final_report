ten-en-chi-hou
ten-en-chi-hou is an ancient chinese view of the universe which considers the heaven as round and the earth as square.
this idea has been used as a motif of buildings and ornaments of chinese cultural sphere.
the heaven has been expressed as round because the movement of stars are represented as circular motion.
