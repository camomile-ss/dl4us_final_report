tomonari iwakura
tomonari iwakura(1668-1680)was a retainer of imperial court who lived during the first half of the edo period.
his father was tomoaki iwakura,sangi(councilor).
his mother is unknown.
though being one of the heads of the iwakura family,he died young at the age of thirteen.
his last official court rank was only jugoinoge(junior fifth rank,lower grade).
the line of the iwakura family was inherited by noritomo iwakura,the adopted son who was a son of arikore chigusa.
