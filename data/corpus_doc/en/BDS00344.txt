genchi
genchi(1183-january 18,1239)was a priest of the jodo sect of the early kamakura period.
his father was taira no moromori.
he was the founder of murasakino-monto(believers).
he was also called myoho-in hoin.
his posthumous name was seikanbo.
he worked as a close aide of honen,and when honen was dying,he was given "ichimai-kishomon" (literally,a one-sheet written pledge).
while he revived chion-in temple in 1234,he established the basis of hyakumanben chion-ji temple.
he left the work "senchaku yoketsu."
