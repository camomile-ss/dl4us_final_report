sengen-zukuri shrine construction
sengen-zukuri is one of the architectural styles of shrines in japan.
summary
sengen-zukuri is a two-story architectural style in which a sanctuary,the main building of a shinto shrine,is built on top of another sanctuary.
examples
fujisan hongu sengen-taisha shrine
-honden(the main hall)was built in the sengen-zukuri style
shizuoka sengen-jinja shrine
-haiden(the front shrine)was built in the sengen-zukuri style
tamagawa sengen-jinja shrine
-honden(the main hall)was built in the sengen-zukuri style
