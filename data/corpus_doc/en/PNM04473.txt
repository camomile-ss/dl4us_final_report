tameto reizei
tameto reizei(september 1846-august 1894)was a son of tameyuki reizei.
his mother was a daughter of akikuni kato.
the 17th family head of the shimo-reizei family
his younger brother was fujimaro(adopted son of meishi sakuramoto),and his younger sister was the wife of masayoshi myoshoji.
his wife was眉延子,the oldest daughter of sanenaru ogimachisanjo(sanenaru saga).
his child was tameisa reizei.
on january 21,1853,he was appointed jugoi(junior fifth rank).
on july 7,1884,he was ranked as viscount.
served as jiju(a chamberlain).
