hanamikoji-dori street
hanamikoji-dori street is a street running east-west through kyoto city.
hanamikoji-dori street spans a length of about 1 km from sanjo-dori street at its north end to yasui kitamon-dori street at its south end,and is the main street passing through the central zone of gion.
the section of hanamikoji-dori street south of shijo-dori street started to develop during the meiji period with tea rooms and restaurants lining the road.
