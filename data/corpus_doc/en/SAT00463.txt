myoju-ji temple(gojo city)
myoju-ji temple is a temple of the nichiren-shoshu sect located in gojo city,nara prefecture.
its sango(honorific mountain prefix)is jodosan.
origin and history
december 22,1973-it was founded.
the patron of the temple in its founding was nittatsu shonin,the 66th head priest of nichiren-shoshu taiseki-ji temple.
december 10,1981-the ossuary was built.
address
2-23,5 chome,futami,gojo city,nara prefecture
surrounding area
yoshino river
saito lumber company
gojo-futami post office
access
ten-minute walk from yamato-futami station on wakayama line
