nenbutsuichie
nenbutsuichie means repeating a buddhist invocation,i.e.namu amidabutsu(a single,sincere call upon the name of amida).
how to chant
in the case of aimauchi(interval drumming),beat mokugyo(a fish-shaped wooden drum)at o.
beat mokugyo strongly at ●,softly at o and moderately at △.
