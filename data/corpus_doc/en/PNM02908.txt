taira no morizumi
taira no morizumi(dates of his birth and death are unknown)was a samurai-daisho(warrior who gives the order of battle and maneuvers the troops)of the taira family in the end period of the taira clan government.
he was known as a hangan(judge)of settsu province.
he was appointed a kebiishi(officials with judicial and police powers)in 1168.
he subdued the ishikawa family of the kawachi-genji-line along with minamoto no suesada.
later,he fought battles at various places in saigoku(western part of japan).
he was captured during the battle of dan-no-ura and transferred to kamakura.
