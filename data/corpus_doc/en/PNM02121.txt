shigeyoshi mori
shigeyoshi mori(1916-1991)was a nohgakushi(noh actor)playing waki(supporting roles)of hosho-ryu school.
he was a son-in-law of arata hosho,the 11th head family of hosho-ryu school and yaichi hosho was his brother-in-law.
he was designated as an individual holder of important intangible cultural property(living national treasure).
tsuneyoshi mori was his child.
he established a reputation in the world of nohgaku for his beautiful voice and the beauty of utai(the chanting of a noh text).
on february 6,1991,the day after he appeared in the noh play "kocho" (butterflies)in national noh theatre,he died suddenly during sleep.
