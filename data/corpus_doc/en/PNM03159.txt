makoto tokugawa
makoto tokugawa(october 31,1887-november 11,1968)was the ninth son of yoshinobu tokugawa,the 15th shogun of the edo bakufu.
his mother was sachi nakane,a daughter of yoshisaburo nakane.
he was a baron.
his wife was seiko nawa,a daughter of baron nagayoshi nawa,and his son was osamu tokugawa.
he became a cadet branch of the yoshinobu tokugawa family and was baronized on november 5,1913.
he worked for yokohama specie bank.
he later became a member of kizokuin(the house of peers).
