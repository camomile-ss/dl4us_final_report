takakazu washinoo
takakazu washinoo(1606-october 5,1662)was a court noble of the early edo period.
he was an adopted son of takanao washinoo.
his natural father was kanekatsu hirohashi.
the kakaku(family status)is the urin family.
he served as shonii gon dainagon(provisional chief councilor of state at senior second rank).
his lawful wife was a daughter takanao washinoo.
among his children,takamitsu washinoo,takatada washinoo,a daughter who became the second wife of yoritaka sagara,tenchoin(the wife of hiroyoshi kikkawa),a daughter who became the wife of sanematsu hashimoto,takakura no tsubone who served tofukumonin as churo(female housekeeper of middle rank),are known.
