higechaya-oiwake
higechaya-oiwake was a point in the yamashina basin,where otsukaido road diverges from tokaido(it was also called yamashina-oiwake).
it is the present-day border between otsu city and kyoto city.
when referring to tokaido gojusan-tsugi(fifty-three stages in tokaido road),tokaido does not head towards sanjo ohashi bridge from otsu-juku station,but turns southwestward,and headed to fushimi-juku station(kyo kaido road).
nearest station
oiwake station(shiga prefecture)on the keihan keishin line of keihan electric railway
