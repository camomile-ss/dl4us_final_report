fujiwara no yoshitoki
fujiwara no yoshitoki(year of birth and death unknown)was a nobleman approximately during the mid heian period.
he successively held the position of assistant governor of musashi province.
in the anna incident in march 969,he worked with sama no suke(vice-minister of left division of bureau of horses)minamoto no mitsunaka to overthrow sadaijin(minister of the left)minamoto no takaakira and others.
