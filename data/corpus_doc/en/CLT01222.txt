kamaburo
kamaburo is a kind of japanese traditional sauna baths existing around yase,sakyo ward,kyoto city.
people enter a huge kamado(furnace)shaped like a kamakura(hut made of snow)and take vapor bath.
at present,kamaburo is available in "furusato" and "heihachi-jaya".
see also "yasedoji".
kamaburo is a famous confection of kyoto which has been modeled after the kamakura-shaped kamado explained above.
it is a thin layer of wheat-flour dough shaped like kamaburo with bean jam stuffed inside.
