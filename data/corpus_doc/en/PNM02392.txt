sumio yamada
sumio yamada(1937-)is a noh actor,shite-kata(main roles)of kongo school.
he came from kagoshima prefecture,became a disciple of tatsuya okuno in 1960 and started to perform for the audience the programs including "stone bridge," "dojo-ji temple," "ataka" and "mochizuki "(the full moon,noh play).
he established jyunsei-kai(純星会literally meaning a group of pure stars,which was organized as an amateur group)in 1985 and jyunsei-kai(潤星会literally meaning a group of abundant stars,which was organized as a professional group)in 1992.
he lives in tokyo.
