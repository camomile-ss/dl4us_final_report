jujo station
jujo station(tokyo),located in the kita ward of tokyo,is a station on the saikyo line(akabane line)of the east japan railway company(jr east).
jujo station(kintetsu railway),located in the minami ward of kyoto city,is a station on the kintetsu kyoto line.
jujo station(kyoto municipal subway),located in the minami ward of kyoto city,is a station on the kyoto municipal subway karasuma line.
the station of kintetsu railway and the station of koto municipal subway are situated distantly.
