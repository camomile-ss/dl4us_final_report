tamemoto reizei
tamemoto reizei(1854-1905)was a japanese kajin(waka poet).
tamemoto was the 21st head of the kami-reizei family.
tamemoto's father was tametada reizei.
on july 7,1884,tamemoto was given a title of count.
tamemoto served as daiguji(the supreme priest)of ise-jingu shrine from 1898 to his death.
tamemoto had a son named tametsugi reizei.
major written work:
"saiten-saho zen" (all the manners for ceremonies)
