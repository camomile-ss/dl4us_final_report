tsunenori nijo
tsunenori nijo(1286-date of death unknown)was a kuge(court noble)who lived during the kamakura period.
his biological father was noriyoshi nijo
his foster father was morotada nijo.
one of his biological brothers was motonori nijo.
he held the rank of shonii(senior second rank).
he served as a non-councilor
brief personal history
tsunenori was born the son of noriyoshi nijo in 1286.
on february 19,1293,he was promoted to the rank of jusanmi(junior third rank).
he entered the priesthood on october 12,1321.
tsunenori's age at death is unknown.
