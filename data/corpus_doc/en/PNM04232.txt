tadasuke ichijo
tadasuke ichijo(year of birth and death unknown)was kuge(court noble)in the kamakura period.
he was the fourth son of sanetsune ichijo.
his mother was the daughter of fujiwara no sadataka.
his brothers included saneie ichijo,moronaga ichijo,ietsune ichijo and iefusa ichijo.
he was junii(junior second rank).
he was hisangi(an advisor at large).
brief personal history
he was born in kyoto as a son of sanetsune ichijo.
he was conferred jusanmi(junior third rank)in july 27,1283.
he entered into priesthood in august,1290.
therefore,the year of his death is unknown.
