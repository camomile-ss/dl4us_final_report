kunimitsu hirohashi
kunimitsu hirohashi(july 8,1526-december 10,1568)was a court noble during the sengoku period(period of warring states)(japan)and the azuchi-momoyama period.
he belonged to the hino line of the northern house of the fujiwara clan,his kakaku(family status)was rated as an distinguished family(member of the court noble),and a son of kanehide hirohashi.
his mother was a daughter of masaaki kajuji.
his wife was a daughter of nagaie takakura.
his children included kanekatsu hirohashi,terusuke hino,nisshin(16th chief priest of honkoku-ji temple and first chief priest and founder of jojakko-ji temple).
he called himself goinjoin(後引接院).
his posthumous buddhist name was sonjaku(尊寂).
his official rank was shonii gon dainagon(senior second rank,provisional chief councilor of state).
he served emperor ogimachi.
