masatoki kusunoki
masatoki kusunoki(the date of birth unknown-february 4,1348)was a military commander during the period of the northern and southern courts(japan).
he was the second son of masashige kusunoki and a younger brother of masatsura kusunoki.
in 1346 in the battle of shijonawate,he fought against the brothers ko no moronao and ko no moroyasu and was defeated,as a result,he committed suicide together with his older brother masatsura by stabbing each other.
