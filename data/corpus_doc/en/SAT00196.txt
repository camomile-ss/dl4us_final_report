zuiko-ji temple(kyoto city)
zuiko-ji temple is a buddhist temple belonging to the nichiren sect located in fushimi ward,kyoto city.
it is also known as gensei-an temple.
history
the temple began as a thatched hut erected on this site by nissei(motomasa)in 1655.
