yamato-oji-dori street
the yamato-oji-dori street is a street running north-south in kyoto city.
it runs from sanjo-dori street in the north to sennyuji-michi(a road south of ikkyo elementary school)in the south.
it corresponds to the former nara-kaido road.
the section north of shijo-dori street is called nawate-dori street(nawate--a bank of the kamo-gawa river(the yodo-gawa water system)).
facilities along the street
kennin-ji temple
hoko-ji temple
toyokuni/hokoku jinja shrine(kyoto city)
kyoto national museum
sanjusangendo temple
