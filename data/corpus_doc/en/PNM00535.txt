fujiwara no hamao
fujiwara no hamao(year of birth not known;died on september 1,840)was a court noble who lived in the heian period.
he was the third son of fujiwara no manatsu of the northern house of the fujiwara clan.
his mother was from the mikuni clan.
his rank was jugoinoge(junior fifth rank,lower grade),and minbu shoyu(junior assitant minister of popular affairs).
he had children including fujiwara no iemune,fujiwara no kadomune,fujiwara no sadanori,fujiwara no akinaga(藤原飽永)and fujiwara no masutomo(藤原益友).
he served three emperors,i.e.the emperor saga,the emperor junna and the emperor ninmyo.
