tatsunosuke onoe
tatsunosuke onoe is a kabuki(traditional drama performed by male actors)actor.
the stage family name is otowaya.
the jomon(family crest)is yotsu-wa ni daki-gashiwa(four rings with a pair of japanese emperor leaves).
