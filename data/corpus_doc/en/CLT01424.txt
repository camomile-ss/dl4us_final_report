kigo(to write with a brush)
kigo,or揮毫in chinese characters,means writing words or phrases with brushes.
the word derived from a japanese phrase, 'fude o furuu' (毫を揮う,meaning to wield a writing brush).
summary
celebrities and calligraphers often write the saying and draw words on the sign on request.
many of these calligraphies are traded at a high price by those who write with brushes.
writings by the emperors and imperials are called 'ofude' and treated with respect.
