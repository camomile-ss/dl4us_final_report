anpukuden hall
anpukuden hall was one of the halls of the dairi(imperial palace)in the city of heian-kyo.
situated in the southeast of the imperial palace,this hall was located to the south of kyoshoden hall(palace archive hall)beyond gekka-mon gate.
it stood opposite shunkyoden,which was situated on the eastern side of the south garden,facing in an east-west direction.
it was also called kusudono(physician's hall)because court physicians and medical students resided there.
