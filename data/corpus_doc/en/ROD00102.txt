magohashi-dori street
magohashi-dori street is a street running east-west through the western part of sakyo-ward,kyoto city.
the street is one block north of sanjo-dori street and runs from shintakakura-dori street in the east to kawabata-dori street in the west.
sometimes the eastern extension in furukawamachi-dori street is called magohashi-dori street.
also,sometimes the street to the east which runs from furukawamachi-dori street to jingumichi road is called sanjokitaura-dori street.
