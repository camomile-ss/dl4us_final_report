manzaburo umewaka(the second)
manzaburo umewaka the second(march 23,1908-april 21,1991)was a noh actor of the kanze school of shite-kata(lead actors).
his former name was masayo.
he was the fourth son of manzaburo umewaka the first.
he succeeded to the name of manzaburo in 1948 and became the head of the manzaburo umewaka family.
manzaburo umewaka the third is his eldest son.
masaharu umewaka is his second son.
