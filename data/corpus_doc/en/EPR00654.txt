princess yamatototo wakaya hime
princess yamatototo wakaya hime(yamatototo wakaya hime no mikoto;her date of birth and death are unknown)was a princess of the emperor korei.
princess yamatototo wakaya hime(倭迹迹姫命)was also written in this way,yamatotobihaya wakaya him(倭飛羽矢若屋比売).
the mother of princess yamatototo wakaya hime was yamato no kuni kahime.
her maternal half-brothers were princess yamatototo himomoso hime and prince hikoisaseribiko.
the emperor kogen was his paternal half-brother of princess yamatototo wakaya hime.
there were no written records on the achievements of princess yamatototo wakaya hime.
