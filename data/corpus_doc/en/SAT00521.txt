kawata-jinja shrine(konan city)
kawata-jinja shrine is a shinto shrine located in kosei-cho,konan city,shiga prefecture.
it is a shikinaisha ronja(a shinto shrine considered to be a descendant of shrines listed in engishiki laws).
the enshrined deity
amenokoyane no mikoto
history
the first enshrinement date is unknown.
it is a shrine considered to be a descendant of shrines registered in the list of deities under engishiki laws.
the crest for the shrine
mitsudomoe
festival
reisai,may 1
