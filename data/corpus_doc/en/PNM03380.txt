nobukane oda
nobukane oda(?-1583)was a japanese military commander who lived during the sengoku period(the period of warring states).
he was commonly called shinhachiro.
nobukane was the third son of nobuyuki oda,who was a younger brother of nobunaga oda.
nobukane was a nephew of nobunaga.
nobusumi tsuda,the oldest legitimate son of nobuyuki,was an older brother of nobukane.
nobukane served nobutaka oda,a son of nobunaga.
immediately after the honnoji incident,nobutaka premeditatedly murdered nobusumi tsuda,a real older brother of nobukane,but nobukane kept serving nobutaka.
nobukane followed nobutaka to the grave.
