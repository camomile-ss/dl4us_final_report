ominari hekino
ominari hekino(date of birth and death unknown)was a government official(lower or middle ranked)in the nara period.
his surname was miyatsuko.
later,he took the name of ominari torii.
career
in 777,he was conferred torii sukune(third highest of the eight hereditary titles).
