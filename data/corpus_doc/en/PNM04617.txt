fujiwara no tarashiko
fujiwara no tarashiko(year of birth unknown-july 2,794)was a female nobles in the nara period.
her father was fujiwara no momokawa and her mother was fujiwara no moroane.
although she became a wife of imperial prince ate(later emperor heizei),she died of illness in 794 that fell on the year of the transfer of national capital to the city of heian-kyo.
in 806,at the time of enthronement of emperor heizei,she was conferred the title of empress posthumously.
