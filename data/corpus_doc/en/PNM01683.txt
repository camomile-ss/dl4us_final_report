hidetaro kataoka
hidetaro kataoka is a kabuki(traditional drama performed by male actors)actor.
the stage family name is matsushimaya.
current hidetaro kataoka is the second.
the jomon(family crest)is nanatsu-wari maru ni ni-hiki(literally,seven percents with two lines and a circle).
hidetaro kataoka(the first)
he later became nizaemon kataoka(the 11th).
hidetaro kataoka(the second)
he is the second son of nizaemon kataoka(the 13th).
