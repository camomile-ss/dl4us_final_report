kintoshi tokudaiji
kintoshi tokudaiji(february 1,1371-august 9,1428)was a kugyo(high court noble)in the muromachi period.
his father was sanetoki tokudaiji.
his go(pen name)was gononomiya.
his buddhist name was tsunetoshi.
brief personal history
in 1371,he was born as the child of sanetoki tokudaiji.
on december 27,1386,he became a sangi(royal advisor).
in 1418,he became a udaijin(minister of the right)(1418-1419).
on february 9,1419,he was appointed to juichii(junior first rank).
in 1420,he became a daijo daijin(grand minister of state).
on july 16,1419,he became a priest.
in 1428,he passed away.
