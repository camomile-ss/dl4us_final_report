sukenobu hino
sukenobu hino(1224-1292)was the 16th head of the hino family.
he was the second son of the 15th family head,iemitsu hino.
his mother was the daughter of fujiwara no tadatsuna.
his son was toshimitsu hino.
he held the office of minbukyo(minister of popular affairs).
he wrote "ninbuki " (sukenobu's diary)during a period from 1246 to 1279.
