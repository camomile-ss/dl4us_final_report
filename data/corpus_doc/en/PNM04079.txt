mitsuyoshi terada
mitsuyoshi terada(date of birth and death unknown)was busho(japanese military commander)in azuchi-momoyama period.
he was harima no kami(the governor of harima province)
mitsuyoshi terada served hideyasu toyotomi,an adopted child of hidenaga toyotomi,and was given 15000 koku(crop yields)in yamato province
after hideyasu's sudden death in may,1594,he served hideyoshi toyotomi while ruling his domain as it was.
in the battle of sekigahara in 1600,he belonged to the west squad,and joined the battle of fushimi-jo castle,so that he forfeited his position and properties(kaieki),and there was no further information about him since then.
