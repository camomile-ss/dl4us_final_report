masutomi geology museum
masutomi geology museum is a foundation located in kamigyo ward,kyoto city.
it operates a museum of stones.
summary
history
in 1973,it was inaugurated as japan geologic study hall.
in 1991,the hall became a foundation and its name was changed to masutomi geology museum.
major business:the operation of a museum of stones(the wonder stone museum)
location:394 naka-demizu-cho,demizu-dori karasuma-nishi-iru,kamigyo ward,kyoto city,kyoto prefecture
