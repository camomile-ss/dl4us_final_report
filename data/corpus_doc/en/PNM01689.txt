tokizo nakamura
tokizo nakamura is a kabuki(traditional drama performed by male actors)actor's professional name.
the stage family name is yorozuya kinnosuke yorozuya(it was harimaya up to the fourth).
the jomon(family crest)is kiricho(paulownia leaves arranged in the shape of a butterfly)and the kaemon(alternate personal crest)is tsuru katabami(cuckooflower with vines).
the name of tokizo originated from tokizo namino,the real name of the first.
