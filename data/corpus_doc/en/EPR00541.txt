amako no iratsume
amako no iratsume(year of birth and death unknown)was a daughter of munakata no kimi tokuzen,a gozoku(local ruling family)of chikushi munakata-gun county.
she was a hin(an imperial wife of the lowest rank)of emperor tenmu.
she was the mother of prince takechi(takechi no miko).
she was also called 'shoko iratsume.'
