soga no oane no kimi
soga no oane no kimi(year of birth and death unknown)was the daughter of soga no iname.
she had a sister,soga no kitashihime,and a brother,soga no umako.
she was the empress of emperor kinmei and the mother of umaragi no miko(prince umaragi),katsuragi no miko(prince katsuragi),anahobe no hashihitohime(princess anahobe no hashihito),anahobe no miko(prince anahobe)and hatsusebe no miko(prince hatsusebe)who became the emperor sushun.
