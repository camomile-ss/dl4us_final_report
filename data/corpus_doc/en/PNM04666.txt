naganari ogasawara
naganari ogasawara(november,1859-january,1895)was a chamberlain of the crown prince for emperor meiji and emperor taisho.
his father was nagamori ogasawara.
his wife was michi,the second daughter of okitaka tanuma.
he had children including keiichi ogasawara(a member of the house of peers)and reiko(toshikata doi's wife).
he was the peerage having a rank of viscount and took part in drafting regulations of viscount society at the first assembly after meiji restoration.
