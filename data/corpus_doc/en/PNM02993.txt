tetsugoro ito
tetsugoro ito(1840-june 20,1868?)from kyoto,yamashiro province,was a member of the shinsengumi.
he served as a corporal.
later,he served as a commander.
he joined the shinsengumi in 1864.
it is said that during the boshin war,he participated in the battle of toba-fushimi and the battle of koshu-katsunuma,and he was killed in the battle of shirakawaguchi.
he died at the age of 29.
there is a theory that he died on intercalary april 25.
among nobori nakajima' paintings,ito was painted when he was fighting in shirakawa.
