shoku shui wakashu
shoku shui wakashu is the 12th imperial anthology of waka(japanese poetry)by the command of the retired emperor kameyama in 1276.
20 volumes.
the compiler was tameuji nijo.
it consists of about 1500 poems by poets including the retired emperor gosaga,fujiwara no tameie,fujiwara no sadaie,saneuji saionji,fujiwara no toshinari,and fujiwara no nobuzane.
no preface was provided,but the anthology includes poems by samurai.
