otomo no sakanoue no oiratsume
otomo no sakanoue no oiratsume(date of birth and death unknown)was the first daughter of otomo no sukunamaro and otomo no sakanoue no iratsume,and her younger sister was sakanoue no otoiratsume.
she was a cousin of otomo no yakamochi,and later became his wife.
the japanese characters of her name is also written as坂上大娘.
it is said that her first name is also pronounced 'ohime' or 'ootome.'
there were some poems exchanged between her and yakamochi from around 732 but they later separated.
they reunited in around 739 and she is said to have become the legal wife of yakamochi around the time when the capital was held in the kuni-kyo(740-744).
