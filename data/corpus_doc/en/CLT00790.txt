the koami school
the koami school is a group of maki-e masters engaging in shitsugei(lacquer art).
the koami school and the igarashi school are the two major schools assuming the honorary post of goyo-maki-e(people allowed to provide maki-e to the imperial court).
shirozaemon michinaga toki,a kinju(attendant)of yoshimasa ashikaga,became a priest during the muromachi period and called himself koami.
his descendants adopted the name "koami" as their family name.
the koami family has lasted for nineteen generations.
