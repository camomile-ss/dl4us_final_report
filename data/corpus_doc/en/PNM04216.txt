sukeie kujo
sukeie kujo(october 11,1769-july 24,1785)was a court noble who lived during the edo period.
his father was michisaki kujo.
his mother was kyohime,a daughter of munekatsu tokugawa.
his legitimate son,suketsugu kujo was an adopted(because sukeie had no children).
his court official rank was shonii(senior second rank).
he served as gon dainagon(provisional major counselor).
he called himself rurikoin.
brief personal history
he was born to michisaki kujo in 1769.
he rose to the rank of jusanmi(junior third rank)on march 31,1776.
he died in 1785.
