kyoto international exhibition hall
kyoto international exhibition hall is an event site located in sakyo-ku ward of kyoto city,kyoto prefecture.
it was designed by architect kiyoshi kawasaki.
it is commonly called 'miyako messe.'
operated by:
kyoto industrial promotion center corporation
location
9-1 okazaki seishoji-cho,sakyo-ku ward,kyoto city
phone:075-762-2630
access
it is about ten minutes on foot from higashiyama station of kyoto city subway tozai line.
