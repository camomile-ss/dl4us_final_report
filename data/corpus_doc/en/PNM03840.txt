roppeita kita
the 16th roppeita kita(december 6,1924 to-),is a noh actor of the shite-kata kita school(one of the five schools of shite-kata[main roles]).
the 16th headmaster of the head family in the kita school.
the present head of the family.
his previous name was nagayo.
the eldest son of minoru kita.
he learned from his grandfather,roppeita kita(14th)and his father.
sadayo kita is his younger brother.
