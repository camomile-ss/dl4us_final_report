masaaki kusunoki
masaaki(正顯)kusunoki was a busho(japanese military commander)in muromachi period.
he also used kanji正顕(masaaki)for his name.
he was a son of masakatsu kusunoki..
he was the founder of ise kusunoki clan.
after he lost in oei war,he was rescued by the force of the southern court and moved to kanbe,ise province(mie prefecture).
since then masaaki's lineage settled in the land.
