o kikuchi
o kikuchi(1847-june 15,1868),from the hirosaki domain,mutsu province,was a member of the shinsengumi.
he was also referred to by other names,ei,eigoro,or tanomu.
he was born as the third son of a feudal retainer of the hirosaki clan.
he joined the shinsengumi in 1867,and after he was defeated in the battle of toba fushimi and the battle of koshu-katsunuma during the boshin war,he went to aizu via nagareyama.
although he performed a feat defeating taisho(major captain)of the new government army in the battle of shirakawaguchi,he was killed in this war.
he died at the age of 22.
