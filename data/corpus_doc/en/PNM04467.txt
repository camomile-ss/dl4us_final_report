ki no osadamaro
ki no osadamaro(755-june 825)was a government official in the beginning of the heian period.
according to his family tree,he was the son of ki no inate,the sagami no kuni no suke(assistant governor of sagami province).
in 808,he was appointed to jugoinoge(junior fifth rank,lower grade)chikuzen no kami(governor of the chikuzen province).
after that,he worked at various government offices such as the dazaifu(the local government office in kyushu region)and the kunaisho(the imperial household agency).
in 815,he was promoted to jugoinojo(junior fifth rank,upper grade).
he died at the age of 71 in june 825.
his last rank was sani jushiinojo kuni(junior fifth rank,upper grade courtier without post order).
