ayabe-onsen hotspring
ayabe-onsen hotspring is located in ayabe city,kyoto prefecture.
access
it takes approx.thirty minutes by car from the ayabe interchange of maizuru wakasa expressway,via chutan koiki nodo(a road for the wide agricultural area of chutan),the obama ayabe line of the fukui prefectural route/kyoto prefectural route 1.
aya bus provides a through service to ayabe-onsen hotspring.
however,you must make a reservation to ride on the bus.
hot-spring water quality
the water includes natrium and bicarbonate.
the water temperature of the hotspring source:approximately 18.6 degrees celsius.
the rate of water flowing out of the hotspring source:approx.12.6 litters/minute
ph:approx.8.1
the hotspring facilities
it is operated by the ryokudo company,a so-called third sector cooperation which is partially funded by ayabe city.
nio,' the name of the facilities,is given after the nio-mon gate of komyo-ji temple,a national treasure,which is located near the hotspring.
address:zainomukai 10,mutsuyori-cho,ayabe city,kyoto prefecture
