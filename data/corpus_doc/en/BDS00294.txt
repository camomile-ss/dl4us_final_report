kakugen
kakugen(1000-september 20,1065)was a japanese buddhist monk of shingon sect in the late heian period.
his father was the emperor kazan.
he studied and became a monk under myokan of daigo-ji temple,and thereafter received kanjo(ceremony to be the successor)from shinkaku and ningai.
in 1030,he was appointed to denpo ajari(a master in esoteric buddhism)and was inaugurated as the chief priest of daigo-ji temple,and subsequently he was conferred the title of gon daisozu(a title of high-ranking buddhist monk).
he was appointed to homu(director of temple affairs)of to-ji temple in 1048,resided at todai-ji temple in 1059 and was conferred the title of gon no sojo(a title of high-ranking monk)in 1062.
