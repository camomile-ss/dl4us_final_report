chiba no kunimiyatsuko
chiba no kunimiyatsuko千葉国造was kuninomiyatsuko(local ruling families in ancient japan)ruled the middle south of kazusa province.
"manyoshu" (the first major anthology of early japanese poetry)describes it as知波国造.
