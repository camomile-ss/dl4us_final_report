teruyoshi ichijo
teruyoshi ichijo(november 28,1756-november 25,1795)was a court noble who lived during the edo period.
he was the son of michika ichijo.
he married princess yoshi,the daughter of shigenori tokugawa and had several children such as tadayoshi ichijo,sanetsugu saionji,and a daughter who married kinosa sanjo.
he became the naidaijin(inner minister)in 1771 and later experienced the udaijin(minister of the right)and the sadaijin(minister of the left),and finally became the kanpaku(chief adviser to the emperor)in 1791.
