tanetsuna kutsuki(the lord of fukuchiyama domain)
tanetsuna kutsuki was the third lord of fukuchiyama domain in tanba province.
he was the fourth load of the kutsuki family in fukuchiyama domain.
he was born on january 9,1171 as the first son of tanemoto kutsuki,the second lord of domain.
because his father died in 1721,he took over as head of the family to become the third lord of domain.
he died on june 4,1726.
his age at death was 17.
because he had no children,his uncle taneharu kutsuki succeeded him.
