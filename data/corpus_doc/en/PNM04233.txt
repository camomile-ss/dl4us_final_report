iefusa ichijo
iefusa ichijo(1270-year of death unknown)was kuge(court noble)in the kamakura period.
his father was sanetsune ichijo.
his mother was the daughter of motouji sono.
his brothers included saneie ichijo,moronaga ichijo,tadasuke ichijo and ietsune ichijo.
he was shonii(senior second rank).
he was hisangi(an advisor at large).
brief personal history
he was born in 1270 as a son of sanetsune ichijo.
he was conferred to jusanmi(junior third rank)in february 17,1290.
his whereabouts after 1325 are unknown.
