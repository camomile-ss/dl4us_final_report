tadasada ichijo
tadasada ichijo(may[the old calendar][between may and june],1862-unknown)was a kuge(court noble)who lived in the late edo period.
he was the third son of tadaosa daigo.
he was adopted by saneyoshi ichijo.
his heir named saneteru ichijo was an adopted son(the seventh son of takauta shijo).
he had an official court rank of jugoi(junior fifth rank).
the year of his death is unknown.
