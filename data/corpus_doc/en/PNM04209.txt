fusahiro takatsukasa
fusahiro takatsukasa(september 6,1710-june 9,1730)was a court noble who lived during the edo period.
his father was iehiro konoe.
his mother was a daughter of kanekazu machijiri.
he was adopted by hisasuke takatsukasa.
he called himself seijorinin.
his court official rank was junii(junior second rank).
brief personal history
he was born to iehiro konoe in 1710.
he was adopted by kanehiro takatsukasa and held various posts such as naidaijin(minister of the center)(1728-1730).
he died in 1730.
