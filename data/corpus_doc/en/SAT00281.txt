myotai-ji temple(kyotango city)
myotai-ji temple is a temple of the nichiren shoshu sect located in kyotango city,kyoto prefecture.
its sango(literally, "mountain name"),which is the title prefixed to the name of a buddhist temple,is shintokusan.
the origin and history
june 9,1982
establishment.
the 67th hossu(head priest)of the nichiren shoshu sect taiseki-ji temple,nikken shonin(the venerable nikken)was kaiki(patron of a temple at its foundation).
