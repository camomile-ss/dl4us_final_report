michisaki kujo
michisaki kujo(july 30,1746-june 27,1770)was a court noble who lived during the edo period.
his father was naozane kujo.
his wife was kyohime,the eighth daughter of munekatsu tokugawa.
sukeie kujo was his son.
he called himself jokoin(盛光院).
brief personal history
he was born in 1746 as the eldest son of naozane kujo.
he served as naidaijin(minister of the center)(1759-1770).
he rose to the rank of juichii(junior first rank)on february 15,1769.
he died in 1770.
