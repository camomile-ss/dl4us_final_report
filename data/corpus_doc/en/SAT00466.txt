bukkaku-ji temple
bukkaku-ji temple is a temple of the nichiren-shoshu sect located in kashihara city,nara prefecture.
its sango(literally, "mountain name"),which is the title prefixed to the name of a buddhist temple,is shotokusan.
origin and history
september 12,1981-founded
the patron of the temple in its founding was nikken shonin,the 67th head priest of nichiren-shoshu taiseki-ji temple.
address
813-1 kawanishi-cho,kashihara city,nara prefecture
surrounding area
niizawa senzuka burial mounds
nara prefectural kashihara high school
access
20 minute walk from kashihara-jingumae station on kintetsu kashihara line and kintetsu minami osaka line.
