soto dairin
soto dairin(1480-1568)was a japanese zen monk and a master of the tea ceremony.
he was the 90th chief priest of daitoku-ji temple.
he was born in kyoto.
he was given the name and title of仏印円証zenji by emperor gonara and shogakufutsu kokushi by emperor ogimachi.
at the request of nagayoshi miyoshi,he founded nanshu-ji temple in sakai as a family temple of the miyoshi clan.
joo takeno shiho(inherited the dharma from a priest master).
moreover,many sakaishu,including dochin kitamuki,sozui asaino,sen no rikyu,sogyu tsuda and miyoshi clan,had been enlightened.
