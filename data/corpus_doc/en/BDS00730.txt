kenshun
kenshun(962-year of death unclear)was a priest of the shingon sect in the late heian period.
his father was fujiwara no kaneie
his azana(popular name)was enrin.
he received the denpo-kanjo(the consecration for the transmission of the dharma)in 1006 at the hokaku-ji temple on mt.kongo in yamato province.
from then on,he practiced kanpo(meditative training to reach enlightenment)and devoted himself to attaining miracles.
