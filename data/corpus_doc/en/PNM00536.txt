heizo sakamoto
heizo sakamoto(year of birth is not clear-january 30,1868)was a member of shinsengumi.
his position was kyokucho-zuki(a staff for the head of shinsengumi).
he joined shinsengumi in response to the recruitment of new members that was conducted in edo around october,1867.
he was listed as kyokucho-zuki in the name list compiled by jingoro yokokura.
after fighting at the battle of toba-fushimi,which started january 3 of the following year,he was killed in the battle of hashimoto on january 30.
he was listed in the name list of gokonomiya-jinja shrine.
