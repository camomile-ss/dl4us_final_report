shimogamohigashi-dori street
shimogamohigashi-dori street is a street running south-north through sakyo ward,kyoto city.
the street runs north from the west end of kawai-bashi bridge on the west shore of the takano-gawa river(kyoto city)to uma-hashi bridge.
the street is located in a residential area.
main facilities on the street
kyoto family court:the east end of aoi-bashi bridge
kyoto city shimogamo junior high school
matsugasaki water treatment plant
