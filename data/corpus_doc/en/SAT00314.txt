saginomori-jinja shrine
saginomori-jinja shrine is a shrine,that is located in sakyo ward,kyoto city,kyoto prefecture.
reisai(regular festival)shichiri festival(also called sanyare festival)is held every may 5.
history
it is said that saginomori-jinja shrine was erected during the jogan era(japan)(in 859-877).
this shrine was ubusunagami(guardian deity of one's birthplace)for seven villages at the foot of mt.hiei,and used to be called the monarch of修学寺.
due to the onin war and other wars,it was relocated to its present place in the early edo period.
