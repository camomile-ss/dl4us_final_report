cloistered imperial prince gyoen
cloistered imperial prince gyoen(years of birth and death unknown)was a cloistered imperial prince during the kamakura and northern and southern court periods.
his father was emperor kameyama.
his mother was sochi no tenji,a daughter of taira no tokinaka.
he entered shoren-in temple in kyoto to become a member of the family of cloistered imperial prince ryojo.
he took over shoren-in monzeki(head priest of shoren-in temple)from his blood brother,cloistered imperial prince jido(1282-1342),and resided in juraku-in.
