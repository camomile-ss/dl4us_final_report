enjaku jitsukawa
enjaku jitsukawa is a professional name for kabuki actor.
the stage family name is kawachiya.
jomon(family crest)is kasaneizutsu and kaemon(alternate personal crest)is itsutsukarigane.
enjaku' is originated from the haimyo(also known as haimei;the term originally refers to a pen name as a haiku poet,but it also refers to a kabuki actor's offstage name which can be used officially and privately)of the former master gakujuro jitsukawa.
enjaku jitsukawa(the first)
adopted son of the owner of shibaijaya(a tea house located within a theater),shobe kawachiya,and his real father was a carpenter in osaka;1831-1885.
enji jitsukawa＝>enjiro jitsukawa the first＝>baiko onoe＝>enjaku jitsukawa the first.
enjaku jitsukawa(the second)
first son of enjaku jitsukawa the first;1877-1951.
enjiro jitsukawa the second＝>enjaku jitsukawa the second.
enjaku jitsukawa(the third)
first son of enjaku jitsukawa the second;1921-1991.
enjiro jitsukawa the third＝>enjaku jitsukawa the third.
