tamemasu reizei
tamemasu reizei(1516-october 2,1570)was a kugyo(high court noble)and a poet who lived in the sengoku period(period of warring states).
he was the head of the reizei family(the kami-reizei family).
his father was tamekazu reizei.
his children were tamemitsu reizei and takamasa shijo(adopted son of takamasu shijo).
he was shonii chunagon(vice-councilor of state at the senior second rank)and served at the minbusho(ministry of popular affairs).
his buddhist name was shukaku.
