suketsugu kujo
suketsugu kujo(october 28,1784-march 6,1807)was a court noble who lived during the late edo period.
his father was sukeie kujo.
his legitimate son was hisatada kujo who was a son of harutaka nijo.
he held the post of gon dainagon(provisional major counselor)and the court official rank of shonii(senior second rank).
brief personal history
he was born in kyoto in 1784.
he rose to the rank of jusanmi(junior third rank)on june 17,1794.
he died in 1807.
