sakanoue no otoiratsume
sakanoue no otoiratsume(dates of birth and death unknown)was the second daughter of otomo no sukunamaro and otomo no sakanoue no iratsume.
it is said that she was the second daughter of the sakanoue clan.
otomo no sakanoue no oiratsume was her older sister.
there was a verse in a poem exchanged between otomo no surugamaro and sakanoue no iratsume that implied the engagement of surugamaro and otoiratsume.
