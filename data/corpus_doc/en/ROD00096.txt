higashi kuramaguchi-dori street
higashi kuramaguchi-dori street is a street running east-west in kyoto city.
the street runs from kamihate-cho which is on a junction of three roads with shirakawa-dori street in the east to shimogamohigashi-dori street which is across the tadekura-bashi bridge of takano-gawa river(kyoto city)in the west.
kuramaguchi-dori street is interrupted from shimogamohigashi-dori street to the east gate of kamo mioyaj-inja shrine.
main roadside facilities
kyoto university of art and design,kamihate-cho(shirakawa-dori street)
eizan electric railway-chayama station(kyoto prefecture)slightly toward to north
