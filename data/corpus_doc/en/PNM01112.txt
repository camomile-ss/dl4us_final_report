minamoto no norimasa
minamoto no norimasa(dates of birth and death unknown)was a noble who lived in the late heian period.
he was the third son of dainagon(chief councilor of state)minamoto no shigemitsu of daigo-genji(minamoto clan).
career
he was tajima no kuni no kami(governor of tajima province),but he illegally collected land taxes from shrine territories.
furthermore,because of a fight with associates of iwashimizu hachiman-gu shrine in the province,he was finally exiled to tosa province in 1037.
there is no information on his whereabouts after that.
his wife was a daughter of minamoto no norisada.
his daughter became imperial prince atsuhira's wife and she gave birth to princess keishi.
