densho
densho(books on the esoterica-also written as傳書)is a manuscript or book of secret techniques of schools of japanese art and martial arts.
it could be called a type of textbook or a manual nowadays,but was not written for beginners or for general public.
it is a manuscript that included the structure of that school,oral lesson,methods,or secret techniques that granted to an heir or the one who received mokuroku(a certificate of the completion of the art)or a permit from the master.
there is also the shito-ryu school of karate school that renew densho and pass it down.
