fujiwara no yukitsune
fujiwara no yukitsune(1012-november 30,1050)was a court noble and calligrapher who lived during the mid-heian period.
he was the third son of dainagon(chief councillor of state)fujiwara no yukinari.
he was held the rank of junii(junior second rank)and served as sangi(councillor)and hyobukyo(minister of hyobusho ministry of military).
he was the second head of the sesonji school of japanese calligraphy.
