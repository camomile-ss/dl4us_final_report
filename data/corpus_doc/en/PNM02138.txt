kuro shigefusa hosho
kuro shigefusa hosho(july 2,1900-july 18,1974)was a noh actor of the hosho school of shite-kata(lead actors).
the 17th head of the hosho school.
he was a son of kuro tomoharu hosho,the 16th head of the school.
he was a member of the japan art academy.
he trained many capable disciples.
fusao hosho was his son.
