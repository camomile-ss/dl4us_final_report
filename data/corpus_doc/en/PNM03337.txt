jozan miizumi
jozan miizumi(1805-november 4,1877)was a priest born in bizen province(present-day okayama prefecture).
he was born as the second son of the chief priest at famous myoon-ji temple in bizen province in 1805.
at the age of 48,he moved to hokkaido.
in 1866 when he was 66 years old,jozan miizumi found a hot spring source at jozankei onsen,present-day in minami ward,sapporo city and opened toji ba(therapeutic bath)there.
