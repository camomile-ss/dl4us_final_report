nobuyuki matsudaira
nobuyuki matsudaira was the fifth lord of the kameyama domain,tanba province.
the tenth family head of the katahara-matsudaira family in the kameyama domain,tanba province.
on september 3,1785,nobuyuki was born as the first son of tsunetaka matsudaira,the branch family of the lord of the kameyama domain,tanba province and taishin hatamoto(a greater vassal).
in 1802,when the fourth lord nobutaka matsudaira died at an early age,nobuyuki succeeded to the position of family head as an adopted son of nobutaka to become the fifth lord,and was conferred to jugoinoge(junior fifth rank,lower grade),the governor of kii province in december.
died on may 11(also said may 14),1816.
died at the age of 32.
his seventh son nobuhide succeeded to his position.
