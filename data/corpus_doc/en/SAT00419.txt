koto-sanzan(the three temples in the east of lake biwa)
koto-sanzan(the three temples in the east of lake biwa)collectively means three tendai sect temples,namely saimei-ji temple,kongorin-ji temple,and hyakusai-ji temple.
they are located in the east of lake biwa and the west side of the suzuka mountain range,and are known as beautiful colored autumnal leaves together with eigen-ji temple in higashiomi city.
