itsutsuji-dori street
itsutsuji-dori street is a street running east-west in kyoto city.
itsutsuji-dori street runs westward from a point on omiya-dori street to the point on onmae-dori street in front of the east gate of kitano tenmangu shrine in kamishichiken.
main facilities along itsutsuji-dori street
kyoto municipla karaku junior high school
senbon-shakado shakyamuni hall(daihoon-ji temple)
kamishichiken
kitano tenmangu shrine
