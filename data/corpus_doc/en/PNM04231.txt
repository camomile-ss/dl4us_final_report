moronaga ichijo
moronaga ichijo(1258-december 5,1293)was kugyo(a court noble)in the kamakura period.
he was a son of sanetsune ichijo.
his brothers included saneie ichijo,ietsune ichijo,tadasuke ichijo and iefusa ichijo.
he was shonii(senior second rank).
he was hisangi(an advisor at large).
brief personal history
he was born in 1258 as a son of sanetsune ichijo.
he was conferred jusanmi(junior third rank)in december 24,1274.
he died in 1293.
