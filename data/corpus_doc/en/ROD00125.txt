jofukuji-dori street
jofukuji-dori street is a street running south-north through kyoto city.
this street runs south from kenkun-jinja shrine on funaokaminami-dori street,and turns at rozanji-dori street and teranouchi-dori street,heading south from in front of jofuku-ji temple to takeyamachi-dori street.
bojo-dori street is located at the southern extension.
main facilities on the street
kenkun-jinja shrine
shonen-ji temple(neko-dera temple):teranouchi-dori street agaru
jofuku-ji temple:ichijo-dori street agaru
kyoto city seishin elementary school:ninnaji kaido
kyoto city nijokita elementary school:sawaragicho-dori street
