kinyuki sanjo
kinyuki sanjo(1105-july 16,1148)was a kugyo(top court official)at the end of the heian period.
he was part of the kanin line of the northern house of the fujiwara clan.
he was the second son of saneyuki sanjo.
he was jusanmi(junior third rank)and sangi(councillor).
