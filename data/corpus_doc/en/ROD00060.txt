takakura-dori street
the takakura-dori street is a street running north-south in kyoto city.
it corresponds to the takakura-koji street of heiankyo.
it runs from marutamachi-dori street in the north to the takakura rikkyo overpass(connected with the takeda-kaido road),but is interrupted halfway by shoseien.
main facilities along the street
the museum of kyoto
kyoto nishiki food market
daimaru kyoto
bukko-ji temple
takakura-kaikan hall
shoseien
shogyoin(saru-dera temple)
