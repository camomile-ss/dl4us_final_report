tametada reizei
tametada reizei(july 26,1824-april 25,1885)was a japanese poet.
he was the twentieth head of the kami reizei family.
his father was tametake reizei.
his legal wife was a daughter of takamitsu yanagihara.
his sons were tamemoto reizei and tamemori irie.
his court rank was shonii gon chunagon(senior second rank,provisional vice-councilor of state).
