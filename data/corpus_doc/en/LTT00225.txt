inpumonin no daifu
inpumonin no daifu(ca.1130-ca.1200)was a court lady and poet in the late heian period.
her father was fujiwara no nobunari ranked jugoinoge(junior fifth rank,lower grade).
her mother was the daughter of sugawara no ariyoshi.
she served imperial princess ryoshi(inpumonin),daughter of the cloistered emperor goshirakawa.
she actively participated in karin-en(poetry gatherings of court poets)that shune held in his residence in shirakawa.
she made friends with many poets such as fujiwara no sadaie,jakuren,saigyo and minamoto no yorimasa.
her poems were included in the imperial anthologies from 'senzai wakashu' (the collection of a thousand years)onwards and compiled a collection of her own verses, 'inpumonin no daifushu.'
hyakunin isshu(hundred poems by one hundred poets)
