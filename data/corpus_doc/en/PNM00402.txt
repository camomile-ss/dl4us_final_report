eisuke okuzawa
eisuke okuzawa(the date of birth unknown-july 8,1864)was a member of shinsengumi.
okuzawa was a corporal.
it is unknown from which clan he was from.
when the ikeda-ya incident happened,okizawa,who belonged to the troops led by isami kondo of shinsengumi,rushed into ikeda-ya with them,was seriously injured,and died at a stationing facility of shinsengumi.
aizu clan gave a reward of 20 ryo to okuzawa.
okuzawa's grave is located in mibu-dera temple.
