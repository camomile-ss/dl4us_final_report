harechika tsuchimikado
harechika tsuchimikado(january 15,1788-august 4,1842)was a court noble who lived during the late edo period.
his father was yasuhide tsuchimikado.
his children included haruo tsuchimikado and fujiko tsuchimikado.
his court official rank was shonii(senior second rank).
he remained a hisangi(advisors at large).
he was an onmyoji(master of yin yang).
he served in onmyoryo(bureau of divination).
brief personal history
he was born to yasuhide tsuchimikado.
he rose to the rank of jusanmi(junior third rank)on january 24,1821(december 21,1820 in old lunar calendar).
he died in 1842.
