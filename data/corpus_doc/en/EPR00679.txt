nitabe no himemiko(princess nitabe)
nitabe no himemiko(year of birth unknown-october 26,699)was a princess of the emperor tenchi.
her mother was tachibana no iratsume(whose father was abe no uchimaro).
her older sister-uterine was asuka no himemiko(the princess asuka).
when she died in 699,an imperial edict ordered that the imperial family members,the vassals and all the officials to attend her funeral.
