harumitsu hino
harumitsu hino(february 20,1518-october 13,1555)was a court noble in the sengoku period(period of warring states).
his father was uchimitsu hino.
terusuke hino was his adopted child.
his buddhist name was shogaku.
he was shonii(senior second rank),gon dainagon(provisional chief councilor of state).
brief personal history
in 1518,he was born as the child of uchimitsu hino.
on march 10,1539,he became a sangi(royal advisor).
in 1555,he passed away.
