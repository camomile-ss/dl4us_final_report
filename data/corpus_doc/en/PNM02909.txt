munakata no ushishi
munakata no ushishi was a local ruling family in munakata(present munakata city,fukuoka prefecture).
munakata(宗形)can also be written as '宗像,' '胸形,' '胸方,' or '胸肩' in chinese characters.
he is believed to be a grandchild or great-grandchild of munakata no tokuzen.
it is said that his son was akiyo.
