toshisada bojo
toshisada bojo(1251 or 1252-december 25,1310)was a court noble in the kamakura period.
he was the originator of the bojo clan.
his father was tsunetoshi yoshida.
his mother was a daughter of taira no narimitsu.
his buddhist name was bojo dainagon(major counselor).
brief personal history
he was born as a son of tsunetoshi yoshida in 1251.
he established the bojo family later.
he became a sangi(councillor)on july 11,1288.
he became a priest on august 28,1306.
he died in 1310.
