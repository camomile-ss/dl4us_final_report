koga-ji temple
koga-ji temple had existed near shigaraki no miya in the mid nara period.
it corresponded to gokoku-ji temple at present.
the great buddha was to be built here,however,a wave of natural disasters changed the plan to build it in todai-ji temple in nara.
the site of the temple is the site of shigaraki no miya,kise,shigaraki-cho town,koga city,shiga prefecture.
